The Hunger Games: Catching Fire

In the second installment of the Hunger Games series, Katniss and Peeta are forced to return to the arena to compete against previous victors in a special edition of the games known as the Quarter Quell, all while facing political turmoil and rebellion in the districts.

La La Land

La La Land follows the love story of an aspiring actress and a struggling jazz musician in Los Angeles as they pursue their dreams and navigate the challenges of their relationship and careers.

IMDB Rating: 8.0/10

Toy Story 3

In Toy Story 3, Andy's toys face a new adventure as they are accidentally donated to a daycare center and must escape the clutches of the tyrannical Lots-O'-Huggin' Bear to make their way back to their owner before he leaves for college.

Indiana Jones and the Last Crusade

In "Indiana Jones and the Last Crusade," Indiana teams up with his father to find the Holy Grail before the Nazis do. Along the way, they face challenges and puzzles, confront old enemies, and learn more about each other.
